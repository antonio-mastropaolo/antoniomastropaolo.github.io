The AURA Lab

(A)I for (U)nderstanding and (R)esponsible (A)utomation in Software Engineering

The AURA Lab, established in Fall 2024, is dedicated to address key software engineering challenges at the intersection of sustainability, automation, and interpretability. Our mission is to develop innovative methodologies and tools that not only enhance the efficiency and reliability of software systems but also ensure they are environmentally responsible and easily understandable.
